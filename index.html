RECIPE FOR LASAGNA

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian pasta dish made by layering wide, flat noodles with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta mixture, and plenty of melted cheese. To prepare it, ground beef is first sautéed with garlic and onions, then simmered in tomato sauce seasoned with herbs like oregano and basil. In a baking dish, cooked lasagna noodles are layered with the meat sauce, a mixture of ricotta and egg (or béchamel sauce), and shredded mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. These layers are repeated until the dish is full, finishing with a generous topping of cheese. The lasagna is then baked in a preheated oven until bubbly and golden on top. After resting for a few minutes, it is sliced and served warm, offering a comforting and flavorful meal perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

Ingredients

Lasagna Noodles: are wide, flat pasta sheets that are used to create the layers of the dish.
Ground Beef: serves as the main protein and gives the lasagna a rich and savory flavor
Onion: adds aroma and enhances the overall taste of the meat sauce.
Garlic: provides a strong and fragrant flavor that deepens the taste of the sauce
Tomato sauce: forms the base of the lasagna and adds moisture and tanginess
Ricotta Cheese: creates a creamy layer that balances the meat and sauce.
Mozzarella Cheese: melts between the layers and on top to give the dish a soft and stretchy texture.
Parmesan Cheese: adds a sharp and slightly salty flavor that enhances the overall taste of the lasagna.

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a large pan, heat olive oil and sauté chopped onions and garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add the ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess oil if needed.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, and seasonings such as salt, pepper, oregano, and basil. Let the sauce simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with an egg and a little salt.
  7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom of a baking dish.
  8. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  9. Spread a layer of the ricotta mixture, then add meat sauce, and sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  10. Repeat the layers (noodles, ricotta, meat sauce, cheese) until all ingredients are used, finishing with cheese on top.
  11. Cover with foil and bake for about 25–30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 10–15 minutes until the top is golden and bubbly.
  12. Let the lasagna rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing and serving.
